The path of sorcery

Upon choosing the path of the sorcerer, Olaf introduces you to Alan, who will oversee your training.

Greetings young sorcerer to be. I am Alan, Master Sorcerer and the one who shall oversee your training. You are probably asking yourself what magic I used upon you as you were entering my sorcerer's tower. Well I was embuing you with the energies required to work such magic. In order for you to access this new power of yours however, we must first strengthen your mind.

I have prepared a mental exercise for you that is especially designed to do just that.

Upon passing this test, the knowledge you will have acquired in doing so, will have sufficiently strengthened your mind.

Begin!
