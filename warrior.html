The way of the warrior

Upon choosing to become a Warrior, Olaf introduces you to Steve, who will oversee your training.

Greetings young warrior. My name is Steve and it is my task to make a great warrior of you. In times gone by we would begin by strengthening your body, until it is a mortal weapon, after which we would focus on strengthening the mind that wields it, by teaching you skills in swordsmanship and other lethal weapons.

However, times are changing and our order prides itself on changing with them. Noone fights with a sword these days and we must all either adapt, or perish. Behold, the latest in weapons technology. It is called a keyboard.

Once you have typed 100 characters, you will have acquired both the strength to wield it, and the skills to master it.

Begin!
